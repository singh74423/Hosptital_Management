Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new patient?

Navigate to the Patients page and click the "Add Patient" button. Fill in the required information and click Save.

How can I schedule an appointment?

Go to the Appointments page, click "New Appointment", select a patient and time slot, then save the appointment.

How do I view analytics?

Visit the Analytics page to view charts and reports about patient admissions, recovery rates, and other statistics.

Can I export my data?

Yes, go to Settings and use the "Export Data" button to download your data as a JSON file.
